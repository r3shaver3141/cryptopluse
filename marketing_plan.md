Marketing plan draft for crypto scrape data package

Audience
- Advanced retail traders
- Professional funds and institutions

Value proposition
- Clean, time-stamped crypto signal data with deterministic, audit-friendly logging
- Ready-to-ingest CSV/JSON exports for backtesting and automation
- Lightweight digest options for operational awareness

Product surface
- Data package (CSV+JSON snapshots)
- Per-coin volume/spike logs
- Paper-trades mock engine for validation
- Watch-list alerts (future real delivery)

Pricing (starter tiers) and discount
- Plan A–D with 20% annual discount as requested
- Clear licensing terms and usage boundaries

Go-to-market steps
- Build a landing page with data samples
- Create a technical appendix (data dictionary, schema)
- Offer onetime pilot + annual contract option
- Collect feedback and adjust features/pricing.
